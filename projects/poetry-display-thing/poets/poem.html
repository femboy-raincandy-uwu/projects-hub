Poem Title: “The Midnight Hour”

Author: Alex Rain

The clock strikes twelve, the night is deep,
The stars above begin to weep.
In silence draped, the world stands still,
While shadows climb the windowsill.

A whisper floats from moonlit trees,
A song that rides the midnight breeze.
The heart takes flight, the soul ignites,
In dreams reborn through silver nights.
